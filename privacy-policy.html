LaneLens Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

LaneLens is a replay review app for iPhone and iPad. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is used.

Data We Collect

LaneLens does not require an account and does not use advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or tracking technologies.

The app stores replay files, replay analysis data, match records, app preferences, and related metadata locally on your device so that you can import, review, and manage replays.

Network Services

When you use match lookup, following, or replay download features, LaneLens may contact public match metadata and replay download services. These requests may include public match IDs, public player/account IDs, and ordinary technical information needed for network requests, such as your IP address as processed by those third-party services.

LaneLens does not sell this information and does not use it for advertising or user tracking.

Feedback Email

If you choose to send feedback from the app, the message is sent through the Mail app. Your email address and message content are shared with the developer only because you chose to send that email.

Data Retention and Deletion

Replay files, processed replay data, and preferences are stored locally on your device. You can remove local replay data from within the app where available, or remove app data by deleting the app from your device. Feedback emails are retained as needed to respond to support requests.

Children's Privacy

LaneLens is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when app features or data practices change. The updated version will be posted on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact: cz5nl@outlook.com

LaneLens 隐私政策

最后更新日期：2026 年 5 月 3 日

LaneLens 是一款适用于 iPhone 和 iPad 的录像复盘应用。本政策说明本 App 会处理哪些数据以及这些数据如何使用。

我们收集的数据

LaneLens 不要求创建账号，不使用广告 SDK、第三方分析 SDK 或追踪技术。

为了让你导入、复盘和管理录像，本 App 会在你的设备本地保存录像文件、录像分析数据、比赛记录、应用偏好设置和相关元数据。

网络服务

当你使用比赛查询、关注玩家或录像下载功能时，LaneLens 可能会访问公开的比赛元数据和录像下载服务。这些请求可能包含公开的比赛 ID、公开的玩家/账号 ID，以及网络请求所需的普通技术信息，例如由第三方服务处理的 IP 地址。

LaneLens 不会出售这些信息，也不会将其用于广告或用户追踪。

反馈邮件

如果你选择在 App 内发送反馈，邮件会通过系统“邮件”App 发出。你的邮箱地址和邮件内容只会因为你主动发送邮件而提供给开发者。

数据保留与删除

录像文件、处理后的录像数据和偏好设置保存在你的设备本地。你可以在 App 内删除本地录像数据（如相关功能可用），也可以通过删除 App 来移除本地应用数据。反馈邮件会在处理支持请求所需的期限内保留。

儿童隐私

LaneLens 并非面向 13 岁以下儿童，也不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。

政策变更

如果 App 功能或数据处理方式发生变化，本政策可能会更新。更新后的版本会发布在本页面。

联系方式

如有隐私问题或删除请求，请联系：cz5nl@outlook.com
